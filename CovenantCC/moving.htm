We're Moving!

Sunday School Connect at Covenant Church is a place where the Bible is shared in a setting of fellowship and friends. There are wonderful teachers for every age prepared to share God's Word as it relates to the needs of everyday life and experiences that everyone faces. This is a relaxed atmosphere and a slower pace where relationships can be built with your Covenant family. Psalm 92:13 says those who are planted in the House of the Lord shall flourish. Come and grow with us in the Word during this time of worship, Word, and discipleship.

Sunday School Connect is at 6pm on Sunday evenings at Covenant church following the church schedule. There is a place for all ages. Elementary age will be connecting with other children while growing in God's Word. Twenty20 youth has a connect group where teen issues are the focus and God's Word is the answer. COED, college and young adult, has a connect group where the Word of God gives direction for our future and forgives our past. Adult connect groups are a place where life testimonies and the Word gives hope and revelation to each other (choose the adult class that is right for you).

There is a Sunday morning connect for people 55 and older. It will be from 9:30am-10:15am. (childcare is not provided)
