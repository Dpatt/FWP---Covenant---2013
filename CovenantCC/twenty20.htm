Twenty20

Twenty20's desire is that we become young men and young women who love, know, and desire to make Him, Jesus, known to others.  We are a generation for Jesus Christ. It is our commitment to you that we provide a creative, real, and relevant environment for you to grow in God's Word. Come join Covenant Twenty20 youth and change the world around you with God's amazing Love.

WATCH OUT Twenty20

Twenty20 has been about helping students experience The Real God in a real way.  Every Wednesday night Douglas gets twenty20 vision for students becoming complete in Christ.  This corporate setting allows students to express their relationship with Christ through live energetic worship, multimedia screen  content, Word, and challenged to become the spiritual giants God created them to be.. Watch out for all the great things happening in Twenty20 by keeping up with Covenant events.
