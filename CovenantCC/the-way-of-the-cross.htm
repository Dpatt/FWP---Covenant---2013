The Way of The Cross

John 14:6 Jesus told him, “I am the way, the truth, and the life. No one can come to the Father except through me.

Romans 10:9" If you confess with your mouth Jesus as Lord, and believe in your heart that God raised Him from the dead, you shall be saved."

  • GOD LOVES YOU:GOD IS NOT MAD WITH YOU
  • SIN SEPARATES PEOPLE FROM GOD
  • JESUS DIED FOR YOUR SINS
  • YOU CAN RECEIVE JESUS NOW
  • AND KNOW GOD'S LOVE

Pray this prayer out loud:

Heavenly Father, in Jesus' name I repent of my sins and open my heart to let Jesus come inside of me. Jesus, You are my Lord and Savior. I believe you died for my sins and you were raised from the dead. I believe that you came to give me abundant life. Fill me with your Holy Spirit. Thank You Father for saving me in Jesus' name. Amen.
