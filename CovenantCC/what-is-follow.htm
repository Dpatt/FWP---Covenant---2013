What is follow?

Get pumped up for follow! A three day youth conference (grades 6th - 12th) that offers out loud Worship with live music. Listen to our dynamic speakers as they shared through God's word the meaning of being a follower of Jesus Christ, and how God has transformed their lives as they've followed His Word. Join together with people just like you wanting to live life as Jesus did, and learn how your life can be the only form of God's Love that your generation sees.

What will I need?

All participants must be registered, If you haven't already, register for follow here! Along with registering you'll need to bring some basic items. You can find a list of what you need to bring here or on the link below. Please make sure to have a copy of your insurance card with you as well as a signed & completed Medical Release Form. What's is it going to cost?

The total price for participants is $150 and includes all meals, travel & lodging. Payments can be made with Check, Cash, or you can pay by credit card on our Registration form here.

When is follow?

June 6th 7th & 8th

Where is follow?

All participants will be bused from Covenant Church. The bus will be leaving on Thursday, June 6th at 9am and returning on Saturday, June 8th at 3pm.
