Kidztown is a high energy Sunday morning church for children Preschool -5th grade.  With break out groups for preschool, a Kidztown experience will have your kids wanting to come back for more.  From the moment kids enter our facilities and experience a creative environment, they will leave exposed to a GREAT BIG GOD with a heart of endless love for each one of them! Join us 10:30 AM during Sunday Service.
If KidzRock and Kidztown are a full meal deal, then KIDZ BLITZ is dessert. KIDZ BLITZ is high energy and full of activities. The focus of KIDZ BLITZ is character building and life application of Bible truths. Isaiah 54:13 "And thy children shall be taught of the Lord; and great shall be the peace of thy children."  Kidz Blitz takes place during Wednesday night service at 7:00 PM with a breakout group for preschool.
KidzRockis a great time of fellowship, Word and fun while children are connecting to each other, their leaders and the Father God.  Kidzrock will take a scripture every month and a virtue word that children can build their character on.  Children are being taught that Jesus is the Rock!!  Kidzrock meets on Sunday nights and follows  the Connect Group schedule.  Sundays at 6:00 PM.
Sunday SchoolThere is a class for every age group.  The focus of this program is Bible Truth and learning the Word of God.  Sunday morning at 9:30 AM.

To the parents

At Covenant Church, we believe that the Father God uses parents to disciple and instruct their children. We also believe it is our job to come along side you and build a team that encourages our children to grow in the Word of God. We challenge our children and parents to open the Word and read it together while learning that Jesus is the same yesterday, today and forever. You are the greatest voice in your child' life whether you think they are listening or not. "Do not grow weary in well doing, for in due season you will reap a harvest." Galatians 6:9
