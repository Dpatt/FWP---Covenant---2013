July!!! What a wonderful month. Celebrating FREEDOM! John 8:36 says if the Son makes you free, you shall be free indeed. You have been made free but how do you understand your freedom... you understand it by staying in the Word. Jesus was the Word made flesh, He is the Truth, and He is Freedom. It says in Joshua 1:8 to be strong and courageous and to meditate on the Word day and night so that you may be prosperous and have good success.

We had a wonderful time celebrating July 4th at Scott & Dawn Meeks home. We had great food, fireworks and fellowship. We also will be having backpack day later in the month and are so excited to be joining with businesses in our community as we serve the people of Coffee County. As a church you are giving 2000 backpacks into your local children! WOW!

 

Storms! The new series with Pastors Gregory and Jackie Pope for July!

We look forward to the STORMS series at Covenant church. This series deals with the storms of life and shares that their is one that calms every storm. Jesus is  peace in the middle of the storm.  It says in Psalm 107:9 He calms the storm, so that its waves are still. It is time to speak to your storm... Peace be still in the name of Jesus.
