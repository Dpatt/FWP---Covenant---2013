Graduation Month and School is coming to an end for summer! May is a month of new beginnings. We have already had lots of great things happening at Covenant this month. There was a Men's Meeting on May 1 and then Dean Sikes shared with us on May 2nd bringing a Word of Hope. The "YOU MATTER" campaign has been launched and we are so thankful to be a part of it. Job 33:4 say God's Spirit made me and gave me life. God created you as an original. God created you for relationship. God created you to fulfill a purpose. Connect with YOU MATTER on twitter @deansikes, facebook You Matter or www.deansikes.net

On May 13th, we will be recognizing graduates during the 10:30am service. There will be no PM service on May 13th because it is Mother's Day! There is a graduation banquet (invitation only) on Tuesday, May 15th for graduates and their families.
VBS Covenant Champions is May 21, 22 and 23 from 6pm-8pm at Covenant church. Please sign up in the lobby and check out our commercial on Covenant Church facebook page.
One of the Bible stories we will be sharing is from 1 Samuel 17. David and Goliath! Realizing that we are champions and can defeat the Goliaths in our lives with the Word of God! Learning to take all our plans and all our decisions and let Jesus give us direction. Phil. 4:13 You can do all things through Christ Jesus who gives you strength. Look to Jesus, follow Jesus, believe in Jesus, and Joy becomes automatic.
