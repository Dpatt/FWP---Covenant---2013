Happy Thanksgiving

{tag_recipientfirstname},

We love the holiday season. It is a great time to remember the goodness of our Savior Jesus! Everyday should be Thanksgiving! It says in the Word in 1 Thessalonians 5:18 in everything give thanks.We are always looking for the next thing but Thanksgiving reminds us to slow down and be thankful for the now. Be thankful for where you are at and know that the Lord has great things for your future. He wants to do above and beyond what you can ask, think or imagine.  No matter who you are or where you are remember their is a loving Father God that will never leave nor forsake you. God's Love is abundant! Come and eat from His banqueting table of Love, Peace, Hope, Forgiveness and Joy today! Also, remember that there is no mid-week service on Wednesday, November 21st and No PM service on Sunday, November 25th. We look forward to seeing you at Sunday school at 9:30am and then for Sunday Service at 10:30am.
